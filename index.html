Introduction In this tuturiol, I will anaylze the relationship between school enrollments and county and time. By anaylzing this dataset, we can test our hypothesis to see if there is a dependence relationship between county and enrollment rates and overall if there is an trend in school enrollments. The following dataset is from https://opendata.maryland.gov/api/views/9ju3-j8k6/rows.csv?accessType=DOWNLOAD, sourced from Maryland State Department of Education. Data are grouped by Pre-Kindergarten (Pre-K), Kindergarten through Grade 5 (K-5), Grades 6-8, and Grades 9-12. Data is from 2009-2019.

    df[grade] = grouped[grade].dropna().reset_index(drop=True)
df['Year'] = years
for g in grades:
    df.plot.scatter(x='Year', y=g,title=g)
    #regression line
    m,b = np.polyfit(df['Year'],df[g],1)
    plt.plot(df['Year'], m*df['Year'] + b)
